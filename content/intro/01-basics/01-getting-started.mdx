Welcome to the **BdOI Guide**! This is a comprehensive guide to help you prepare for the Bangladesh Olympiad in Informatics.

# Introduction

Hello, awesome people! If you are reading this, you likely already have some idea of what the BdOI is. If you don't, don't worry-the next two lessons will cover exactly what competitive programming is, how coding competitions work, and the significance of the BdOI and IOI.

Keep in mind that there is no single, rigid guide to mastering competitive programming, just as there is no magic formula for acing the BdOI. This guide is simply here to point you in the right direction. *You* are the engine driving this journey. 

Be obsessed with the process and give it everything you have. Sometimes it will break you, and other times it will bring you immense joy. But if you start from scratch and stay consistent, the journey will completely reshape how you think and teach you profound lessons about resilience and life. 

Enough pep talk. Before we dive in, always remember one thing:

**OI >>>> Anything**
